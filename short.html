Short Courses

In this technology driven era, acquiring new skills is essential to take full advantage of the opportunities available. One of the most important skills is ICT (Information and Communication Technology), which covers a wide range of fields such as computer science, software engineering, web development, data analysis, and more. ICT skills can help you boost your career prospects, enhance your productivity, and solve complex problems. Whether you want to learn a new programming language, create a website, or analyze data, ICT skills can help you achieve your goals.

Enroll in our short course program

Our 8-week short course program in computer science is designed to help you develop your computational thinking and problem-solving skills, learn programming, and explore a wide range of practical, technical, and thinking skills at your own pace. Our enrolls are led by industry experts who have designed original content to provide you with a holistic computer science education. So, enroll today and be prepared for the future of work.
