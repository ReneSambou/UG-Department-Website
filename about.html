About Us

Welcome to the Future of Computing

At the Department of Computer Science at University of Ghana, you will learn from the best in the field, explore cutting-edge research, and create innovative solutions for real-world problems. Whether you want to pursue a bachelor’s, a minor, or a graduate degree in computer science, we have a program that suits your needs and interests. Ready to join us? Apply now and start your journey with us.

About Us

The Department of Computer Science at UG is one of the top-ranked departments in the world. Here are some facts and figures about us:

  • We have over 100 faculty members, including 7 Turing Award winners, 4 MacArthur Fellows, and 3 Fields Medalists
  • We offer a wide range of courses and research areas, such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, computer vision, natural language processing, robotics, cryptography, algorithms, theory, systems, security, and more.

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science program at UG is designed to provide you with a solid foundation and a broad perspective of the field. You will learn the fundamental concepts, principles, and techniques of computer science, as well as how to apply them to various domains and problems. You will also have the opportunity to choose from a variety of electives and specializations, such as data science, human-computer interaction, bioinformatics, and more. Here are some reasons why you should choose our program:

  • You will be taught by world-class faculty who are leaders in their fields and who are passionate about teaching and mentoring students.
  • You will have access to state-of-the-art facilities, such as labs, libraries, and computing resources, that will enhance your learning experience and support your research projects.
  • You will be part of a vibrant and supportive community of students, alumni, and faculty, who will help you grow academically, professionally, and personally.
  • You will have excellent career prospects, as our graduates are highly sought after by top employers in the tech industry and beyond.

Minor in Computer Science

The Minor in Computer Science program at UG is designed to complement your major and to broaden your horizons. You will learn the basics of computer science, such as programming, data structures, algorithms, and discrete mathematics, as well as how to use them in your own field of study. You will also have the flexibility to choose from a range of electives that suit your interests and goals, such as web development, game design, artificial intelligence, and more.

Here are some benefits of choosing our program:

  • You will enhance your analytical, logical, and problem-solving skills, which are valuable in any discipline and career.
  • You will expand your knowledge and perspective of computer science, which is an integral part of our society and culture.
  • You will increase your employability and competitiveness, as computer science skills are in high demand in the job market.
  • You will enjoy learning and having fun, as computer science is a fascinating and rewarding subject.
